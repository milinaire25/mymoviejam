Movies like Interstellar: 10 mind-bending sci-fi journeys

By MovieJam Crew 5 min read Tags: Sci-Fi · Recommendations

Christopher Nolan’s Interstellar mixes grand-scale space exploration with intimate, emotional stakes. If you walked out wanting more films that blend science, heart, and mind-bending ideas, this watchlist is for you. These titles cover everything from survival epics to philosophical chillers—aka the perfect double-feature partners for Coop and Brand.

10 movies like Interstellar

1. Arrival (2016)

Why you’ll like it: Time, language, and love collide in Denis Villeneuve’s quiet masterpiece—very similar emotional wavelength.

Genre: Sci-Fi · Drama · Where to watch: Netflix / Prime (regional)

2. The Martian (2015)

Why you’ll like it: Pure survival porn on Mars with humor, grit, and a NASA control-room you’ll want to high-five.

Genre: Sci-Fi · Adventure

3. Gravity (2013)

Why you’ll like it: White-knuckle set pieces and emotional catharsis set against Earth’s orbit.

Genre: Sci-Fi · Thriller

4. Contact (1997)

Why you’ll like it: Faith meets physics in this Carl Sagan adaptation—it’s the OG “call your parent from space” movie.

Genre: Sci-Fi · Drama

5. Ad Astra (2019)

Why you’ll like it: A meditative father-son odyssey that trades explosions for introspection.

Genre: Sci-Fi · Drama

6. Sunshine (2007)

Why you’ll like it: A desperate mission to restart the sun, dripping with tension and golden visuals.

Genre: Sci-Fi · Thriller

7. Moon (2009)

Why you’ll like it: Minimalist sci-fi that explores identity, loneliness, and the ethics of cloning.

Genre: Sci-Fi · Drama

8. 2001: A Space Odyssey (1968)

Why you’ll like it: The template for philosophical space films—slow, grand, and unforgettable.

Genre: Sci-Fi · Classic

9. Tenet (2020)

Why you’ll like it: Nolan goes full physics puzzle with inverted time warfare.

Genre: Sci-Fi · Action

10. Inception (2010)

Why you’ll like it: Mind-heist cinema with emotional backbone—classic Nolan energy.

Genre: Sci-Fi · Thriller
